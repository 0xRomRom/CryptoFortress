Trading Basics

A 'trader' is someone who trades crypto currencies. Their primary goal is to purchase and sell crypto currencies and try to profit off short/long term price fluctuations.

There are many strategies to trading making it a highly complex topic.
To cover the very basics; A trader is typically looking to
get into a "Long" or a "Short" position.

A Long trade refers to a position that is calculated
based on bullish beliefs. A bull is jargon for someone
who is confident a coin price will appreciate.
They will buy 100 coins at 5$/coin hoping to sell the
100 coins the next day at 6$/coin, leaving them with
a 100$ profit.

A Short trade refers to a position that is calculated
based on bearish beliefs. A bear is jargon for someone
who is confident a coin price will depreciate.
They will sell 100 coins at 5$/coin hoping to buy the
100 coins the next day at 4$ a coin, leaving them with
a 100$ profit.
